Albania is one of Europe’s last adventures. There are still unknown destinations to explore and experience in this country.

Why visit Albania ?

If there is a destination that everyone should visit at least once in his or her lifetime, it is Albania. This small country is one of the last hidden gems of Europe and totally worth a look. From the cliffside views of the sea to the deep blue water of the rivers, from the vibrant capital city Tirana to the stunning mountainous landscapes.

What is so special ?

Albania has some of the most beautiful beaches in Europe and even the world. Although the Albanian Riviera is probably the most popular place to holiday in Albania the crowds are nothing compared to other parts of Europe. When you visit Albania, head straight for the Riviera and explore some of the hidden paradises that are waiting to be discovered. With crystal blue water, white sand beaches and cheap beer, you will be happy with your decision to come here again. That's not everything, plenty more to explore from Albanian nature with mountains, rivers, lakes and a lot more.

What about food ?

One of the best reasons why everyone should include Albania in his or her bucket list’s places to see is the food. Albanian gastronomy is excellent, fresh and tasty. You can eat gorgeous dishes in the fanciest restaurants as well as amazing street foods. Albanian cuisine is primarily based on healthy ingredients such as vegetables, fruit, fish, dairy products, and meat. Olive oil is the most popular cooking fat used in traditional Albanian dishes. Herbs are widely used as well, adding distinct and delicious flavor to the food. Garlic is a staple, as are onions, which may be the most popular ingredients in the entire country.
